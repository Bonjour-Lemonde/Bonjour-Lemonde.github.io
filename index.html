Milestones in My Research Career

In 2012

I was selected into the 1th "Leading Figure Program" and became an undergraduate in the Department of Chemical Engineering in Tsinghua University. One of the graduates in our department is President of China XI Jinping.

In 2014

I entered the research group of Professor LIN Zhanglin, where I first learned about synthetic and systems biology and became obsessed with biological regulatory networks.

In 2015

As the team leader, I organized 5 AIDS-related practical activities in 3 years, hoping to understand the current situations of AIDS patients. And shared my experience with the "Goodwill Ambassador" Mrs. PENG Liyuan as a volunteer representative in 2015. These encouraged me doing research for society needs.

In 2017

After Professor LIN moved to South China University of Technology as the dean, I joined the group of Professor LOU Chunbo in the Chinese Academy of Sciences to continue my research, where I learned quantative characterization methods and tried to improve models to predict the regulatory networks.

In 2019

After Professor LOU Chunbo moved to Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, I joined the research group of Professor OUYANG Qi in the Department of Physics in Peking University to continue my research, where I studied network topology design and felt very happy to be immersed in a research atmosphere of multidisciplinary and freedom.
